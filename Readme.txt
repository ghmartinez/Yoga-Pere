# Yoga Pere - An Online Yoga School

Welcome to the Yoga Pere repository! This is the source code for our online yoga school, where you can learn the art of yoga from the comfort of your own home.

## Features
- Wide range of yoga classes for all levels, from beginner to advanced
- Expert instructors with years of experience
- Flexible scheduling to fit your busy lifestyle
- Interactive community for support and motivation

## Getting Started

To get started, simply visit our website at [https://yogapere.com](https://yogapere.com) and sign up for a free trial. From there, you can access all of our classes and start your yoga journey.

## Contributing

If you are interested in contributing to the development of Yoga Pere, we welcome your contributions! Please fork this repository and submit a pull request with your changes.
